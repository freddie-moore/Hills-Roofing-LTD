Comprehensive Roof Installation

At Hills Roofing, we specialize in complete roof installation services across South East London & Kent. With over 10 years of experience and a team of highly qualified professionals, we ensure every installation meets the highest standards of quality and durability.

Our installation process begins with a thorough assessment of your property's structural requirements, followed by detailed planning and material selection. We work with premium materials and proven techniques to deliver roofing solutions that protect your home for decades to come.

From traditional slate and tile installations to modern flat roofing systems and specialized applications, our comprehensive range of services covers every roofing need. Each project is completed with meticulous attention to detail, ensuring weatherproofing, energy efficiency, and aesthetic appeal.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a roof installation take? +

Installation times vary depending on the roof size, complexity, and materials. A typical residential roof installation takes 3-7 days. We'll provide a detailed timeline during your consultation.

What materials do you recommend? +

Material selection depends on your property type, budget, and preferences. We'll assess your specific needs and recommend the most suitable options, from traditional slate and tiles to modern flat roofing systems.

Do you handle planning permissions? +

We provide guidance on planning requirements and can assist with applications where necessary. Most roof replacements don't require planning permission, but we'll advise on your specific situation.

What guarantees do you offer? +

All our installations come with comprehensive guarantees covering both materials and workmanship. Warranty periods vary by product type, with options ranging from 5 to 25 years.

Can you work in all weather conditions? +

Safety is our priority. While we can work in light rain, we avoid extreme weather conditions. We'll reschedule if necessary and keep you informed of any weather-related delays.

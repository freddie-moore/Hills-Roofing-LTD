Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a new roof installation take?

The duration of a roof installation varies depending on the size and complexity of the roof, the materials chosen, and weather conditions. On average, a standard residential roof replacement can take anywhere from 2 to 5 days. We provide a detailed project timeline with every quote.

What is the best roofing material for my home?

The best material depends on several factors, including your budget, desired aesthetic, and the architectural style of your home. Slate and tile are popular for their longevity and classic look, while felt and rubber are excellent for flat roofs. We can walk you through the pros and cons of each option during our initial consultation.

Is a deposit required to start the work?

Yes, we typically require a deposit to secure the booking and cover the initial cost of materials. The exact amount will be clearly outlined in your detailed quote. The final balance is due upon satisfactory completion of the project.

Do you handle the removal and disposal of the old roof?

Absolutely. Our service includes the complete removal of your old roofing materials. We ensure that all debris is disposed of responsibly and that your property is left clean and tidy upon completion of the job.

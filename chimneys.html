Frequently Asked Questions

Do you repair chimney stacks? +

Yes, we repair cracked bricks, damaged mortar, and leaning or unstable chimney stacks.

What is a chimney cowl? +

A chimney cowl prevents downdrafts, improves airflow, and keeps out rain and pests. We recommend one for most chimneys.

How long does a chimney repair take? +

It depends on the extent of the damage, but most repairs take between 1–3 days to complete.

About Us

Hills Roofing LTD is a leading provider of professional roofing services for both commercial and domestic properties across Croydon, South East London and Kent. Founded by Connor Hill, we are committed to delivering exceptional quality, guaranteed workmanship, and unparalleled customer service on every project, big or small.

Why Choose Hills Roofing

We built our reputation on trust and results. When you choose us as your local roofing company, you get a team that is experienced and founder-led, with Connor bringing over 10 years of hands-on expertise to every project. We are fully certified and approved, holding qualifications such as CSCS and SSI training, Bauder Approved Contractor status, Construction Line Gold membership, and Asbestos Awareness certification—giving you complete peace of mind. Most importantly, we back every new installation with robust guarantees so that you can be assured your roof is built to last.
